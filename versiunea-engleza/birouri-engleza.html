Offices

Atmosphere in the workplace is one of the factors that most significantly influences employee satisfaction and productivity. It can be greatly improved through the use of an air freshener with carefully selected scents, inducing a state of relaxation for employees and reducing anxiety. A study conducted in a financial services company found that the use of a cinnamon scent reduced the number of errors made by employees by 40%. In another Japanese study, the effect of a pleasant floral scent was tested while subjects performed arithmetic exercises. The results showed a significant reduction in stress, accompanied by a decrease in skin secretions. Whether dealing with an investor, a new client, or a consumer entering your office, making a good impression is crucial. The use of a suitable fragrance will contribute to a positive experience and eliminate unwanted odors. Recent studies show that people remember what they touch at a rate of 1%, what they hear 2%, what they see 5%, what they taste 15%, and what they smell at a rate of 35%. Therefore, scent will become a signature of the location, immediately recognized by customers.
