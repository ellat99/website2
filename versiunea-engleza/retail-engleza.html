Retail

In the case of supermarkets, hypermarkets, stores, pharmacies, and other retail businesses, differentiation from competitors is essential. However, this often requires additional time, effort, and costs. Fragrance has the power to influence the consumer's state and, consequently, establish an emotional connection with them. Choosing a specific scent that consumers can associate with the store will lead to a unique and memorable experience, one that they are likely to want to repeat. Experiments conducted in clothing stores have shown that diffusing vanilla-scented perfume in areas dedicated to women's clothing doubled sales. Let's imagine entering a children's toy store where it smells like bubblegum, reminiscent of childhood. Then, we enter another identical store where the smell is of plastic from toys. In which store are we more likely to make a purchase? Most people will choose the first option as it is perceived as safer for children.
