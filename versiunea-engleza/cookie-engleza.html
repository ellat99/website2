2. What Are Cookies?

An Internet Cookie (also known as Browser Cookie, HTTP Cookie, or simply Cookie) is a small amount of information used by websites to make their use more efficient. This small file, consisting of letters and numbers, does not contain software programs, viruses, spyware, cannot access information from the user's hard drive, and is completely "passive."

The law stipulates that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies, we need your consent.

3. How Are Cookies Used?

In the context of Web 2.0, there are several roles that cookie modules can have:

  • They allow the customization of settings such as the language in which a site is displayed, the currency in which prices and fees are expressed, and the retention of certain options in the shopping cart.
  • They allow multimedia applications to be part of a specific website, thus enhancing the browsing experience.
  • They provide website owners with feedback on how they are used.

In short, cookies are used to remember user choices at a certain time or to recognize frequent visitors to a website, ensuring a pleasant browsing experience. Cookies do not retain personally identifiable information.

4. What Is the Lifespan of a Cookie?

A cookie can have a validity period (lifespan) ranging from a single session to several years or may even never expire. A user can delete all cookies from their browser at any time.

5. What Types of Cookies Can Be Used?

This site uses various types of cookies. Some cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of this site, while others are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages.

5.1. Necessary Cookies

Necessary cookies help make a website usable by enabling basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies. Cookies classified as necessary will NOT be used for:

  • The collection of information that could be used for promoting products or services to you.

If you block these cookies, our website may not function. To ensure the functioning of our website upon access, we recommend expressing your agreement regarding the use of necessary cookies. These cookies are necessary for the operation of the website.

5.2. Performance and Tracking Cookies

Performance and tracking cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the website by anonymously collecting and reporting information. We use cookies to compile statistics about visitors, such as the number of visitors who have accessed the site, the type of technology they use (which helps us identify when our site is not working correctly with certain technologies), the time they spent on the site, the pages they viewed, etc. This allows us to continually improve the website. Additionally, we can anonymously learn how visitors arrived at our site (which search engine directed them) and whether they are visiting for the first time or have visited before. For this purpose, if you agree to the use of these types of cookies, casaparfumurilor.ro will use Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel.

5.3. Online Chat Cookies

To streamline communication on the casaparfumurilor.ro website, we use cookies for the JivoChat online chat.

6. How Can You Control Cookies?

To control and/or delete cookies as desired, you can access the help section of your browser and choose this setting. However, please note that in some situations:

  • You will no longer be able to view the website.
  • You will no longer be able to authenticate/login to the site.
  • Some services or options may no longer function.
  • The experience will not be the same as when cookies are enabled.
